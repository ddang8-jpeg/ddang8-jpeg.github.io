Caixin Wang

Phd Candidate

Caixin joined the Computational Sensory-Motor Systems (CSMS) lab in Spring 2022. He received his MSE Degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Johns Hopkins University in May 2022. He received his BEng Degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (First Class Honors) from University of Nottingham, UK in 2020. With a pixel-wise exposure variable camera sensor, his currently working at a real-time compressed sensing video reconstruction system and HDR video reconstruction via flow estimation network. He’s going to apply for PhD program starting at Fall 2023.
